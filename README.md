## Introduction

The Simple Shop Web Application is designed to give you a seamless shopping experience. You can browse different product categories, search for products by name, view detailed product information, manage your cart, and complete your purchases through the checkout process.

----------

## Homepage

When you first visit the site, you'll be greeted by the **Homepage**. Here, you can find:

-   **Featured Products**: A selection of products that are featured and available for you to browse.
-   **Product Categories**: A list of all available product categories. You can click on a category to see products related to it.

### How to use the Homepage:

-   **Browse Featured Products**: Click on any product image or name to view more details.
-   **Navigate to Categories**: Click on any category name to view products in that category.

----------

## Product Search(Work In Progress)

The **Product Search** feature allows you to find specific products by name.

### How to use Product Search:

1.  Look for the search bar, typically located at the top of the page.
2.  Type in the name of the product you are looking for.
3.  As you type, the list of products will automatically update to show products that match your search.
4.  Click on a product from the list to view more details.

----------

## Product Details Page

Once you've clicked on a product from the homepage or search results, you'll be directed to the **Product Details Page**.

### What you'll find on this page:

-   **Product Image**: A large image of the product.
-   **Product Description**: A detailed description of the product.
-   **Price**: The price of the product.
-   **Add to Cart Button**: A button to add the product to your shopping cart.

### How to use the Product Details Page:

-   **Add the Product to Cart**: If you wish to purchase the product, click on the "Add to Cart" button. The item will be added to your cart.
-   **View More Products**: Click the "Back to Products" button to return to the homepage or continue browsing.

----------

## Categories Page

The **Categories Page** allows you to view all available product categories.

### How to use the Categories Page:

1.  From the homepage or navigation menu, click on a category name.
2.  You will be taken to the category page where you can see all products in that category.
3.  Click on any product to view its details and add it to your cart.

----------

## Cart Page

The **Cart Page** is where you can view all the items you've added to your shopping cart. You can also update the quantities or remove items from the cart.

### How to use the Cart Page:

-   **View Cart Items**: All the products you've added to your cart will be displayed with their name, price, and quantity.
-   **Edit Quantities**: You can change the quantity of any item in your cart by clicking on the quantity box and entering a new number.
-   **Remove Items**: To remove an item, click on the "Remove" button next to the product.
-   **Proceed to Checkout**: Once you're happy with the items in your cart, click on the "Proceed to Checkout" button to begin the checkout process.

----------

## Checkout Page(Work In Progress)

The **Checkout Page** is where you finalize your purchase. On this page, you will review the items in your cart, provide shipping details, and complete your payment.

### How to use the Checkout Page(Work in Progress):

1.  **Review Your Cart**: Ensure that all the items in your cart are correct and that you are ready to purchase.
2.  **Enter Shipping Details**: Provide the necessary shipping information such as your name, address, and contact details.
3.  **Complete Purchase**: After reviewing everything, click on the "Complete Purchase" button to finalize your order.

----------
